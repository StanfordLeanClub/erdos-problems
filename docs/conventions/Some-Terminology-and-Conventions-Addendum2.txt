More terms and conventions:
- an "ambitious n-combo" / "calibrating n-combo" = an n-combo where on each hit you at a target higher than you think is the next best target. When you report back, say whether the higher targets were generally solvable / progress-able. If the higher targets were generally solvable / yielded about as much or more than we would've seen from the "best" target, then your sense for what's best needs updating to reflect this and the next n-combo should also be ambitious; otherwise, if the higher targets were generally too hard, your sense for the next best target is properly calibrated and the next n-combo should be a normal one.
